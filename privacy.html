Privacy Policy
We are pleased that you are using our application (hereinafter referred to as the “App”). Protecting your privacy is a core principle of Mood Space. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use the App.
1. Data Controller
The data controller within the meaning of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is:
Name: M.R.M Raslan
Email: raslanrauff94@gmail.com
2. Personal Data
Mood Space does not collect or process personal data.
This means in particular:
  • No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or identifiers are collected
  • No accounts or user profiles are created
  • No tracking that can identify individual users takes place
3. Journal Entries & Mood Data (On-Device Only)
Mood Space is designed to be a safe and private space for journaling.
  • Journal entries, mood logs, emotions, and notes are stored locally on your device
  • This data is not transmitted, uploaded, or stored on external servers
  • We do not have access to your journal content at any time
  • Your data remains fully under your control
Your emotional data belongs to you and stays with you.
4. Anonymous Usage Statistics & Crash Reporting
We may collect anonymous usage statistics and crash reports to improve app performance and user experience. This data does not include any personally identifiable information.
The legal basis for this processing is Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR (legitimate interest in improving the App), as no personal data is involved.
